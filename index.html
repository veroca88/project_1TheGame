About the Game

    Where did I put my keys?

  • This game is a training brain game, the objective is get the key, in your way you are going to solve some numerical reasoning tests.
    To move you can use the arrows of the keyboard. ⬅️⬆️➡️⬇️
    For the answer use only numbers.

    Why is numerical reasoning important? Numerical reasoning ability is one of the most basic skill areas, it requires interpreting data provided, is the ability to look for a pattern or a trend and the generalizing it.

    NOTE: For better experience you should play this game in a laptop, desktop or tablet 1220 x 900 or more!
